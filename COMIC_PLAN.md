## Audio — Thomas's Voice

### ElevenLabs voice design prompt
```
Middle-aged Latino man, 50s. Former journalist, calm and precise. Voice: low baritone, slightly husky from years of work. Pacing: deliberate, measured — he chooses every word. Emotional register: controlled grief. The voice of someone telling you something important, keeping their composure. American English, slight warmth in the vowels. No rush. Silences matter.
```

### ElevenLabs settings
- Stability: 0.65
- Similarity boost: 0.80
- Style: 0.25

### Narration pacing notes
- Each sentence = separate paragraph (forces natural pause)
- After "Tuesday." → long pause (0.8s)
- After "Seven-fifteen." → long pause (0.8s)
- After "Eight o'clock." → very long pause (1.2s)
- Em-dash "—" = dramatic pause mid-sentence
- "I am paying attention now." → very slow, almost one word at a time
